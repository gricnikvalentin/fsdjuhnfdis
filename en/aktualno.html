Hand Sanitizer gel, a special offer during the COVID-19 pandemic

4.1.2021

We know that health and safety is one of the most important things during the COVID-19 pandemic, and in light of that we've introduced a special offer for a hand sanitizing gel.
The gel is packaged in 500ml bottles and is suitable for use anywhere, since it has the dosing pump already preinstalled.
It is practical to use, since it has an unique formula, containing both hyaluronic acid and aloe vera, protecting the skin. All that allows the skin to quickly soak up the liquid without it feeling greasy on the hands.

  • Rest homes (Lenart, Velika Polana,...)
  • Schools (Slovenj Gradec, Ravne, Prevalje,..)
